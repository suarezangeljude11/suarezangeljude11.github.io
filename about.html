About Us

Online Clinic Management System is a web-based clinic practice management software designed to easily manage a clinic business anywhere and anytime you need , with the aid of a computer and Internet. It is a very user-friendly, feature-packed software solution that offers superior performance and reliability. It is specifically designed to handle all of your clinic management needs. Solver Online Clinic Management System is ideal for practices of any size, from solo doctors to enterprise class health networks.
